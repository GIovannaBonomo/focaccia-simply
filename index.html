Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow you taste buds with sweet caramleized onion, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will becole you new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 Cups (500gr)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegrar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired,though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually kneas the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
